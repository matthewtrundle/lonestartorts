## Perfect Pairings: Recipes for Anaheim Home Cooks  
1. **Breakfast Tacos**: Scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, and roasted poblano peppers on warm flour tortillas. Top with avocado crema!  
2. **Brisket Tacos**: Shredded smoked brisket, pickled red onions, and chipotle mayo on corn tortillas—ideal for game days.

4. **California-Style Burritos**: Stuff flour tortillas with carne asada, french fries, pico de gallo, and sour cream.
