## Perfect Pairings: Recipes for Irvine Home Cooks  

1. **Breakfast Tacos, Irvine-Style**  
   Scramble eggs with diced Ortega chilies, top with crispy bacon or soyrizo, and wrap in warm Flour Tortillas. Add avocado slices and a dash of hot sauce from Irvine’s own Cholula.  

2. **Brisket Tacos with a Twist**  
   Slow-cook brisket with coffee and chipotle (hello, Texas meets California!), then pile onto Fresh Corn Tortillas with pickled red onions and cilantro. Serve with a side of roasted poblano salsa.  

3. **California Quinoa & Black Bean Quesadillas**  
   Mix cooked quinoa, black beans, and sautéed veggies with Monterey jack cheese. Grill between Butter Tortillas until golden. Cut into wedges and serve with Greek yogurt (a local favorite sub for sour cream!).  

4. **Grilled Fish Tacos**  
   Marinate mahi-mahi in lime and cumin, grill, and layer onto Corn Tortillas with shredded purple cabbage and mango-jalapeño slaw. Perfect for backyard dinners in Irvine’s sunny climate!  

5. **Sweet Butter Tortilla Dessert Rolls**  
   Spread Butter Tortillas with Nutella, sprinkle with sliced strawberries, roll up, and pan-fry in butter. Dust with powdered sugar—ideal for a Newport Beach picnic dessert.
